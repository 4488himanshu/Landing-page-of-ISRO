Our Services

Satalite services

Communication:

ISRO provides satellite-based communication services, including television broadcasting and other telecommunication services.

Weather forcasting:

ISRO operates meteorological satellites that provide data for weather forecasting and monitoring.

Navigation:

ISRO operates the GAGAN and IRNSS/NavIC satellite navigation systems, providing positioning, navigation, and timing services.

Launch Vehicle Development and Services

ISRO develops and operates launch vehicles like PSLV and GSLV to place satellites into orbit. ISRO also offers launch services for customer satellites through its commercial arm, NSIL.

Space Research and Exploration

ISRO conducts scientific research and exploration missions to study space and celestial bodies. This includes missions like the Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan), Chandrayaan missions, and upcoming interplanetary missions.
